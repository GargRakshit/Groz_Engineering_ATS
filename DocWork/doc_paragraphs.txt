'Normal' | Divides raw BM25 by the theoretical maximum score a perfect document would achieve. Returns a value in [0, 1].

'List Bullet' | 8192-token context window (ModernBERT architecture, trained for long documents)
'List Bullet' | Full resume text is passed without truncation — no token budget management needed
'List Bullet' | Applies sigmoid to the raw logit: 1 / (1 + e^−x)
'List Bullet' | LoCo long-document retrieval benchmark score: 90.68; ~300 MB on disk; Apache 2.0
